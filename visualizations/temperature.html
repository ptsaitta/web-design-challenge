Latitude's Affect on Temperature

Broadly, the highest max temperatures are found near the equator and colder temperatures are found at higher latitudes (in magnitude - southern hemisphere latitudes are negative). Interestingly, the correlation is much more pronounced in the Northern Hemisphere than in the Southern Hemisphere. This is likely due to the Southern Hemisphere being closer to the earth during its summer (where max temps would be experienced) than the Northern Hemisphere is during its summer. There is much more water in the Southern Hemisphere too, which may help moderate the temperatures that cities there feel. Of course, latitude and solar flux are only a couple of factors (albeit important ones) that affect temperature and climate.
