Latitude's Effect on Wind Speed

There doesn't appear to be much of a correlation between Latitude and Wind speed either. There appear to be a few data points at higher (in magnitude) latitudes that have higher wind speeds that may imply a direct correlation, but the density of the data points in this region is much lower than that of the other areas, implying a weak relationship at best.
