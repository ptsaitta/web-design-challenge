Latitude's Effect on Cloudiness

There doesn't appear to be much if any correlation between cloudiness and latitude. Additionally, the striation of the data implies that a lot cities record their "cloudiness" measures discretely, with possibilities being 0%, 20%, 40%, 75%, 90%, and 100%. Of course, data points exist between these points, so that is not universally the case.
